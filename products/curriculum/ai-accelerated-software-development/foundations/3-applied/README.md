# Applied Coaching (Weeks 7-10)

Guided coaching where teams apply AI-accelerated development techniques to their actual work projects and establish sustainable team practices.

## Overview

- **Duration:** 4 weeks (Weeks 7-10 of Foundations program)
- **Format:** Team coaching + 1:1 sessions + office hours
- **Teams:** 2-3 teams per cohort
- **Time commitment:** 8-10 hours/week
- **Prerequisites:** Completion of Fundamentals (Weeks 1-2) and Labs (Weeks 3-6)

## Objectives

1. **Apply Tools to Real Projects** — Take lab skills to production codebases
2. **Customize AI Workflows** — Adapt techniques to your specific context
3. **Establish New Ways of Working** — Define team practices and governance

## Key Deliverables

| Deliverable | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Team AI Playbook** | Team's documented rules, practices, and workflows |
| **Capstone PRs** | Real PRs demonstrating AI-assisted workflow |
| **ROI Metrics** | Before/after measurements for leadership |
| **Executive Readout** | Summary presentation of outcomes |

## Week-by-Week Summary

| Week | Focus | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 7 | Transition & Baseline | Identify focus areas, baseline metrics, initial workflow discussion |
| 8 | Workflow Design | First AI-assisted work, draft playbook |
| 9 | Iteration | Refine practices, mid-point review, prepare capstone |
| 10 | Capstone & Readout | Final deliverables, executive presentation, sustainability planning |

## Coaching Model

- **Weekly Team Sessions (2 hrs):** Structured facilitated sessions
- **1:1 Coaching (as needed):** Individual support for specific challenges
- **Office Hours (2 hrs/week):** Open drop-in for questions and pair work

## Success Criteria

**For Teams:**
- Completed Team AI Playbook they believe in
- Demonstrated AI-assisted workflow on real code
- Confidence to continue after pilot ends

**For Organization:**
- Measurable ROI data for leadership
- Replicable model for other teams
- Clear recommendations for broader rollout

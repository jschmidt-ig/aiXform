# AI Accelerated Software Development — Labs Student Schedule (Weeks 3-6)

## Assumptions
- Practitioner tier (Guided Cohort Enablement)
- Expected time: 8-10 hours/week
- Same weekly blocks to create routine
- This covers the Labs phase (Weeks 3-6 of 10-week Foundations program)

## Weekly cadence (repeat each week)

| Day | Time (example) | Activity | Time |
|---|---:|---|---:|
| Monday | 6:00–8:00pm | Watch the week's lecture content + take notes | 2h |
| Tuesday | 6:00–8:30pm | Lab block #1 (hands-on) | 2.5h |
| Wednesday | 12:00–1:00pm | Live cohort session (demo + Q&A) | 1h |
| Thursday | 6:00–9:00pm | Lab block #2 (hands-on) + Office hours | 3h |
| Friday | 4:00–5:30pm | Submit artifact(s) + self-review + plan next week | 1.5h |

**Total:** 8-10 hours/week

## Labs Phase Overview (Weeks 3-6)

| Week | Lab | Focus |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 | Lab 01 | API Foundations |
| 4 | Lab 02 | Codebase Analysis & Documentation |
| 5 | Lab 03 | Testing & Coverage Acceleration |
| 6 | Lab 04 | Observability, Security & Compliance |

## What Comes Next

After completing Labs (Weeks 3-6), learners move to the **Applied Coaching** phase (Weeks 7-10) where they apply these skills to their actual work projects with guided coaching support.

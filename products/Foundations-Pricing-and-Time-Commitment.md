# AI Accelerated Software Development — Foundations
## Pricing & Time Commitment Reference

---

## Program Structure

| Phase | Weeks | Format |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Fundamentals | 1-2 | 20 sessions (lectures + demos) |
| Labs | 3-6 | 4 hands-on labs |
| Applied | 7-10 | Team coaching on real projects |

**Total Duration:** 10 weeks

---

## Pricing (by Delivery Tier)

The 3 phases are bundled as one 10-week program. Pricing varies by delivery format:

| Tier | Name | Price | Format | Description |
|------|------|-------|--------|-------------|
| L1 | Foundations | $300-500/seat | Self-paced, recorded | Learner works through recorded content independently |
| L2 | Practitioner | $2,000-3,000/seat | 10-week cohort | Live cohort with labs and group sessions |
| L3 | Enterprise Accelerator | $30,000-50,000/team | Team coaching | Full engagement with dedicated coaching for 2-3 teams |

### Tier Notes

**L1 (Self-paced)**
- Pre-recorded Fundamentals content
- No live instruction
- Assessment and certification included
- Best for: Individual learners, budget-conscious orgs

**L2 (Practitioner Cohort)**
- Live cohort sessions
- Includes all 3 phases with instructor support
- Group labs and peer learning
- Best for: Individual contributors, small teams

**L3 (Enterprise Accelerator)**
- Dedicated coaching for 2-3 teams
- Full Applied phase with 1:1 and team sessions
- Executive readout and ROI metrics
- Best for: Organizations piloting AI adoption

## Trainer/Instructor Time Commitment

### First Delivery (Content Creation Phase)

Heavy investment while building and refining content:

| Phase | Weekly Hours | Notes |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| Fundamentals (Wks 1-2) | 25-35 hrs/week | Content creation (~5-7 hrs/day) + live delivery (~1 hr/day) |
| Labs (Wks 3-6) | 15-20 hrs/week | Lab creation + live interaction (~2-3 hrs/day) |
| Applied (Wks 7-10) | 10-15 hrs/week | Coaching sessions + support + prep |

**First pilot average:** ~20-30 hrs/week

### Repeat Delivery (Steady-State)

Once content is captured and reusable:

| Phase | Weekly Hours | Notes |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| Fundamentals (Wks 1-2) | ~4 hrs/week | Live delivery only |
| Labs (Wks 3-6) | ~8-10 hrs/week | Live interaction + support |
| Applied (Wks 7-10) | ~8 hrs/week | Coaching sessions + office hours |

**Steady-state average:** ~8 hrs/week

### Trainer Activities by Phase

**Weeks 1-2: Fundamentals**
- Live session delivery (~1 hr/day)
- Content refinement
- Parallel: Lab environment setup, security coordination, tooling approvals

**Weeks 3-6: Labs**
- Live instruction (~2-3 hrs/day across cohorts)
- Lab/exercise support
- Office hours
- Async learner support

**Weeks 7-10: Applied**
- Team coaching sessions (2 hrs/week per team)
- Office hours (2 hrs/week shared)
- 1:1 coaching (30-60 min as needed)
- Executive readout preparation

### Scaling Considerations

| Cohorts | Trainer Load Impact |
|---------|---------------------|
| 1 cohort | Baseline |
| 2 cohorts | ~1.5x (some shared prep) |
| 3+ cohorts | Consider second trainer |

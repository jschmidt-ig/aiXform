Program Overview

Applied is where the workflow moves from safe labs into your real codebase. Teams ship production PRs with coaching support, and leadership receives an executive-ready before/after readout.

Real PRs, Real Impact

Work on your actual repo and backlog. Optimize for smaller, safer PRs with better tests and clearer narration.

Coach-Supported Delivery

Coaching guides planning, constraints, and review discipline without requiring coaches to access sensitive code.

Metrics + Executive Readout

Capture baseline, mid-point, and final deltas on throughput, cycle time, coverage, and review iteration.

Rollout Recommendations

End with a rollout plan: what to scale, what to standardize, and what guardrails are non-negotiable.

What You'll Deliver

Concrete artifacts leadership can review and teams can reuse.

Capstone PR Package

  • 1-3 high-signal PRs per team
  • PR narration (what/why/risk/how tested)
  • Test additions and/or coverage delta
  • Runbook updates where relevant

AI SDLC Playbook v1

  • How the team uses agents (workflow)
  • Prompt policy and redaction guidance
  • Permission model (least privilege)
  • Review and approval gates

Executive Readout

  • Baseline vs final KPI deltas
  • Key wins and failure modes
  • Rollout recommendation
  • Next 90-day plan

Assessment & Reporting

Applied is measured by delivery outcomes, not attendance.

Metrics We Track

  • Cycle time (ticket to merge)
  • PR throughput and PR size
  • Review iteration count
  • Test/coverage delta (targeted)

Governance Checks

  • Prompt injection awareness
  • Least-privilege tool permissions
  • Untrusted input isolation
  • Human review required for merges

Week 10 Readout

  • Before/after KPI comparison
  • What to scale vs what to fix
  • Rollout options and resourcing
  • Operating model recommendations

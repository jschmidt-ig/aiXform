# Research Packet (expected structure from Run 1)

Use this as a checklist to validate the Pro Research output.

1) Executive summary (<=10 bullets)
2) Market map (8–15 comparables) — table with sources
3) Pricing benchmarks — ranges, units, signals, what costs more
4) Packaging patterns — tier structures, gating, delivery models
5) Naming patterns — grouped options with pros/cons
6) Measurement/KPI menu — controllable vs lagging
7) Recommendations — 2–3 packaging models + pricing structures + gross margin targets
8) Risk register — table with mitigations
9) Draft SOW addendum outline
10) PASTE-FORWARD PACKET — 1–2 pages max, clean, pasteable

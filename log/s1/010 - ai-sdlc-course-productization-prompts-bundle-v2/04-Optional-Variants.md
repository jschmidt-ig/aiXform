# Optional variants you can request (copy/paste as add-ons to either run)

## Delivery models to compare
- 10-week, ~1 hr/day cohort model (baseline)
- 6-week, ~2 hr/day accelerated cohort
- 2-week bootcamp, ~6 hr/day intensive
- Hybrid: self-paced + weekly cohort sync + office hours

## Commercial models to compare
- Per-seat time-limited access + optional coaching add-on
- Team packs (10/25/50 seats) + office hours bundle
- Private cohort license (fixed price)
- Enterprise license + training-the-trainer

## Credentialing options
- Completion badge (attendance + labs)
- Practical certification (capstone rubric + observed assessment)
- Internal “champion” track (advanced coaching substitute)
